Richly Divine

Nothing feels better than Chocolate & London Dairy!
  • Serving 24
  • Prep Time 30m
  • Cook Time 8m
  • DifÔ¨Åculty Easy

Ingredients

  • London Dairy Tiramisu Ice cream
  • Brownie mix (or your favorite brownie mix)
  • 2 muffin tins
  • Cooking spray
  • Chocolate sauce
  • Sprinkles

INSTRUCTIONS

Follow the directions for your favorite brownie recipe to make the batter.

  • Spray a muffin tin with cooking spray and add brownie batter to each cup until they're about two-thirds full.
  • Place them in the oven and bake, following your brownie recipe's directions.
  • In the meantime, spray the bottom half of your second muffin tin with cooking spray.
  • Once the brownies are baked, remove from the oven and immediately press the second muffin tin into the brownies.
  • Keep both tins pressed together until the brownies are cool.
  • Add a scoop of London Dairy Tiramisu Ice cream and top it with chocolate sauce and sprinkles for the finishing touch!
